About

Tringle Flight Simulator is a retro-inspired 3D flight game featuring procedurally-generated environments, intuitive mobile-friendly controls, and a minimalist aesthetic. Take control of a tetrahedron spaceship and explore an endless park filled with trees and dynamic terrain.
